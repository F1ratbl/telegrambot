# SEC Filings: 8-K, 10-Q and 10-K

Sources:
- SEC EDGAR: https://www.sec.gov/search-filings
- SEC API documentation: https://www.sec.gov/edgar/sec-api-documentation

Common filings:
- 8-K: Current report for material events such as financing, acquisitions, management changes or major agreements.
- 10-Q: Quarterly report.
- 10-K: Annual report with full business, risk and financial disclosure.

How to interpret:
- Read the filing date and event date.
- Identify the exact event, amount, parties, maturity, rates and conditions.
- Compare management language with previous filings.
- Look for risk factors, liquidity notes and going-concern language.

When explaining news:
- Summarize the event first.
- Explain financial impact and risks.
- Link the filing or company release when available.
