# AI Infrastructure and Data Centers

Sources:
- Company investor relations pages.
- SEC filings: https://www.sec.gov/search-filings

AI infrastructure companies may build or operate data centers, GPU clusters, cloud services or power infrastructure. Revenue potential depends on capacity, utilization, customer contracts, power costs and financing.

Economic interpretation:
- Large AI contracts can improve revenue visibility.
- Data center buildouts require heavy capex and financing.
- Power availability, construction timelines, GPU supply and customer concentration are key risks.
- Equity or convertible debt financing may support growth but can dilute shareholders.

When explaining company news:
- Identify contract size, duration and customer quality.
- Check whether revenue is committed or only potential capacity.
- Discuss capex, funding and execution risk.
